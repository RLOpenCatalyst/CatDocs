 




Organization Setup
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

To start using RL Catalyst, the first step is to setup an organization. While you setup an organization in RL Catalyst, you should also set up the Business Groups and Projects.

**Follow the steps below to create an organization**

 * From the main menu click on Settings
 * Once click on Settings, from the side menu click on Organization Setup
 * Click Organizations
 * Click on +New button provided 
 * On Create Organization page enter Name (Organization Name) and Domain Name ( your website address)

 .. image:: /images/createOrg.JPG


 * Click on Save button
 * Now your Organization is setup and listed in the Organizations page

 .. image:: /images/orgList.JPG



**Edit and Remove an Organization**

You can edit and remove an Organization. Follow the steps to edit and remove an Organization:

 * Click on edit button to edit the organization details
 * Click on delete button to remove the organization from the list


**Activate or Inactivate an Organization**

You can activate or inactivate an Organization by using the cursor button provided.  Remember, once you inactivate the organization all the entries relating to the organization will be disabled

 .. image:: /images/editOrg.png


**Setup Business Groups for an Organization**

In an Organization you can create multiple Business Groups.Follow the steps to setup a new Business Group in an Organization:

 * From the main menu click on Settings
 * Once click on Settings, from the side menu click on Organization Setup
 * Click Business Groups
 * Click on +New button provided 
 * Provide an environment name in the Business Group Name box
 * Select the Organization from the Organization drop down list
 * Click Save button

 .. image:: /images/createBG.JPG

 * Now the Business Group  is setup and listed in the Business Groups page


 .. image:: /images/businessList.JPG

**Edit and Remove a Business Group**

You can edit and remove a Business Group. Follow the steps to edit and remove a Business Group:

 * Click on edit button to edit Business Group details
 * Click on delete button to remove Business Group from the list


**Setup Projects for a Business Group**

In a Business Group you can create multiple Projects.

 * From the main menu click on Settings
 * Once click on Settings, from the side menu click on Organization Setup
 * Click Projects
 * Click on +New button provided 
 * Provide a project  name in the Name box
 * Provide a brief description about the project in the Description box
 * Select the Organization from the Organization drop down list
 * Select the Business Group from the Business Groups drop down list
 * Environments and repository details are not mandatory while creating project, will be explained in later section

 .. image:: /images/createProj.JPG

 * Click Save button

 .. image:: /images/projectList.JPG

**Edit and Remove a Project**

You can edit and remove a Project. Follow the steps to edit and remove a Project:

 * Click on edit button to edit Project details
 * Click on delete button to remove Project from the list
